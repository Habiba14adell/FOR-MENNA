Hi Manouna 🤎

You are probably reading this a day before your big day, your wedding!!! And I just wanted to take a moment to tell you something I don’t think I say, show, or stress on enough.

You truly and deeply mean the world to me, and honestly, I don’t think words will ever be enough to explain my love for you… but this is my little attempt to at least put a smile on your face.

Even though I am aware nothing would match how I feel, you are always the first person that comes to my mind whenever anything happens. You are always on my mind, in the smallest and biggest moments. I love you so much, and I love what we have, our special sacred bond. The kind of relationship people wish they had, as it’s rare and pure. And I always pray that it stays protected, strong, and ours forever.

Seeing you step into this new chapter of your life makes me so incredibly happy. I am just seeing this cute little shy girl walk fiercely into this big world. All I can see is the little Manouna. I’m genuinely so proud of you, for what you have achieved and accomplished. I know you’re going to thrive in this new journey as you have always flourished, because that’s just who you are.

To my rock, kind, strong, loving, and truly one-of-a-kind sister, I just want you to know that I am always going to be right here. I am always going to be your person, your safe place, and your biggest supporter.

I love you always and forever 🤍🤍
